✏️ Correction

Describe the specific corrective action(s) taken to address the identified problem and prevent recurrence. The correction should be stated in past tense and reflect the completion (it's done) date.

🔍 Cause

Identify and document the root cause of the problem that led to the corrective action. Use of 5-Why analysis or other root cause analysis techniques is encouraged to ensure a thorough understanding of the underlying issue. There is a form in \\Common\Quality that should be used to document the 5-Why analysis. The root cause cannot be a restatement of the problem. It should be a specific cause that can be addressed through corrective action.

🌐 Systemic

Document any systemic or organization-wide implications of this corrective action. Identify whether this issue affects other areas or processes. Systemic action shall prevent recurrence of the problem across the organization, not just in the specific instance. If systemic implications are identified, additional actions may be required to address the broader impact and ensure that similar issues do not arise in other areas.
